The most surprising thing in this Portugal property snapshot is just how complete Imovirtual’s dominance is: it accounts for **100% of active listings** in the dataset. In other words, the market isn’t just concentrated — it’s effectively centralized on a single portal.

The numbers make that clear. JobStatsen shows **24,076 active listings** in Portugal, and **all 24,076 new listings added in the last 7 days** also came from Imovirtual. That kind of total control is unusual in real estate, where inventory is typically spread across multiple platforms competing for attention and leads.

For buyers and sellers, this changes the game. Instead of comparing several portals to understand supply and pricing, the visible online market here is essentially one source of truth. That can simplify search and improve efficiency, but it also raises questions about competition and market visibility.
