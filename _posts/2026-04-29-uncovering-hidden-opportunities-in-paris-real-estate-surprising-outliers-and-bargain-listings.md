That means the real story isn’t just high prices — it’s the unexpected pockets where prices fall far below the norm.

In a market as closely watched as Paris, those kinds of deviations are easy to miss if you only look at averages.

The takeaway is simple: Paris is not as flat or predictable as it seems. Median pricing tells part of the story, but the outliers reveal where opportunity may still exist — especially for anyone willing to dig deeper than headline numbers.
