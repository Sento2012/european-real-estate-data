The biggest surprise in Strasbourg’s property market is just how far sale prices are outpacing asking prices. Realty Pulse data shows homes are listed at a median of **€2,500/m²**, but actually sell for **€3,526/m²** — a **29.1% premium**. That’s a clear sign buyers are competing hard, and the market is moving faster than the listings suggest.

Momentum is strong, too: sold prices have climbed **13% in just six months**, putting Strasbourg on the radar for buyers looking for growth without Paris-level pricing. On a typical **70 m² apartment**, the difference between list and sale pricing is huge — about **€175,000** versus **€246,820** — so affordability can disappear quickly once bidding starts.

For buyers, the takeaway is simple: don’t rely on advertised prices alone. Strasbourg looks like a market where the best homes are still commanding serious premiums, which means preparation and speed matter. For sellers, it’s a reminder that well-priced properties may have more upside than expected.
